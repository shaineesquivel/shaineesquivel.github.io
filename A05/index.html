Singapore's Tourist Attraction

Iconic Landmark

Merlion Park

Merlion Park is a top tourist spot in Singapore, known for its iconic lion-fish statue overlooking Marina Bay.

Marina Bay

Marina Bay is a vibrant waterfront area in Singapore, known for its stunning skyline and iconic attractions.

Singapore Flyer

Soaring 165 meters high, the Singapore Flyer gives a stunning bird’s-eye view of Marina Bay and beyond.

Nature and Parks

Garden by the Bay

Gardens by the Bay is a futuristic nature park in Singapore, famous for its Supertree Grove and climate-controlled domes.

Bukit Timah Nature Reserve

A haven for nature lovers, Bukit Timah Nature Reserve offers hiking trails through one of the last primary rainforests.

Mount Faber Park

Known for its lush greenery, Mount Faber Park is a peaceful retreat with walking trails and scenic viewpoints.

Theme Parks

Universal Studio

Universal Studios Singapore is a theme park on Sentosa Island with movie-themed rides and attractions, offering fun for all ages.

Adventure Cove Waterpark

Adventure Cove Waterpark in Singapore offers thrilling water slides, wave pools, and marine life encounters.

S.E.A Aquarium

S.E.A. Aquarium in Singapore is one of the world’s largest aquariums, home to over 100,000 marine animals.

Cultural and Heritage Sites

National Museum of Singapore

The National Museum of Singapore showcases the country’s rich history and culture through immersive exhibits and galleries.

Old Parliament House

The Old Parliament House in Singapore is a historic building, once the seat of government, now serving as the Arts House.

The Bottle Box

The Battle Box is a small underground bunker which was used as a bomb shelter during World War Two.

Shopping and Entertainment

ION Orchard

Ion Orchard is a luxury shopping mall in Singapore, offering high-end brands and stunning views from its observation deck.

clacke Quay

Clarke Quay is a vibrant riverside quay in Singapore, known for its lively nightlife, restaurants, and historic architecture.

Bugis Junction

Bugis Junction is a popular shopping destination in Singapore, blending modern retail with charming street markets and eateries.
